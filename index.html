About

I am a web and software developer with 5 years of professional experience, specializing in front-end development. I have experience working on bespoke applications in .NET and PHP, as well as with content management systems such as Umbraco and WordPress.

I love working on front end stuff and have a lot of experience working in KnockoutJS, jQuery, HTML5 and CSS3. Mostly, I like to make stuff that looks and feels good!
