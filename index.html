RightsApp

RightsApp is an upcoming SaaS Web App I'm working on for Sentric Music. It's aim is to make music publishing as easy as possible for publishers and artists.

RightsApp is built in ASP.NET MVC5, using KnockoutJS, jQuery, Bootstrap and MySQL to automate the process of making royalty claims with performing rights societies around the world for song plays and live performances, as well as dividing royalties between contributors and helping with song placement in TV, film and advertistment soundtracks.

  • Client: Sentric Music
  • Date: April 2016 - Present
  • Service: Web Development

Sentric Music Redesign

I was asked to redesign the Sentric Music brochure site to make it more inviting to potential clients, as well as easier for Sentric Music employees to use the CMS it was based on.

I created new document types, templates and scripting files using Umbraco as well as developing custom plugins to make use of the CMS easier.

I adapted the site to brand guidelines as well as improving responsiveness. The site now adapts to devices of any size.

The response to the redesign was very positive and led to requests for additional redesigns for Connect Songs and Sentric Music Germany.

  • Client: Sentric Music
  • Date: February 2016
  • Service: Web Development

Next Match App

The next match app is a personal project I decided to work on when I wanted an app for my phone to tell me when the next Liverpool game was on with just one tap.

The app is built in Xamarin Forms and uses the Football-Data API to show users the details and time of the next game for their chosen team.

The user can also see details of past and future football matches on a results carousel page and the current league table for their chosen league.

This app is still in development but the Git Repo can be found here.

  • Client: Personal Project
  • Date: May 2017 - Present
  • Service: App Development

TSB

I worked on TSB (Technology strategy Board) in collaboration with Imperial College London to build a web app which predicts future revenue for bands using a combination of their social media data and past claims with performing rights societies.

This was a fun project to work on as it allowed me to experiment with different ways to represent large amounts of data on the web. We used a combination of .NET, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and Highcharts to provide users with their predicted revenue information.

  • Client: Sentric Music and Imperial College London
  • Date: September 2015 - April 2016
  • Service: Web Development

Initio Interactive

Initio Interactive is a company which specializes in corporate hospitality packages and offers VIP experiences at major sport and music events.

They wanted a new site where customers could see and book their event packages, and which could also function as a company blog.

For this project, I built the front end of their new website, from scratch, using Wordpress. I created several custom page types and post categories for Initio's use, as well as a customizable quote area and customer login feature.

  • Client: Initio Interactive
  • Date: June 2013
  • Service: Web Development
